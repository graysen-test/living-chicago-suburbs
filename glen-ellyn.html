About Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is a vibrant village located about 25 miles west of Chicago in DuPage County. Known for its picturesque streets, historic architecture, and welcoming atmosphere, it’s a favorite among families, professionals, and retirees alike.

History

Founded in the 1830s, Glen Ellyn grew rapidly with the arrival of the railroad. Its name comes from Lake Ellyn, a local landmark surrounded by a beautiful park.

Schools

Glen Ellyn is served by top-rated public school districts, including District 41 and District 87 (Glenbard West High School). Several private and parochial schools also serve the community.

Parks & Recreation

The Glen Ellyn Park District operates more than 20 parks, offering residents access to walking trails, playgrounds, athletic fields, and community events year-round.

Downtown & Dining

The downtown area features boutique shops, cafes, and award-winning restaurants. Seasonal events like the Taste of Glen Ellyn and the Holiday Walk draw crowds from across the western suburbs.

Housing

From charming historic homes to modern new builds, Glen Ellyn offers a diverse housing market. Neighborhoods are known for their tree-lined streets and unique architecture.

Transportation

Glen Ellyn has a Metra Union Pacific West Line station for easy access to downtown Chicago, along with convenient highway connections via I-355 and I-88.
