Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is a charming village located just 23 miles west of Chicago. Known for its picturesque downtown, top-rated schools, and strong sense of community, it offers a perfect blend of suburban comfort and city accessibility.

Community & Lifestyle

The heart of Glen Ellyn is its vibrant downtown area, filled with boutique shops, diverse dining options, and seasonal events that bring residents together. Lake Ellyn Park is a local favorite, offering walking trails, picnic spots, and beautiful water views.

Schools

Glen Ellyn is served by highly respected school districts, including Glen Ellyn District 41 and Glenbard Township High School District 87, making it an attractive option for families.

Real Estate

The housing market in Glen Ellyn offers a variety of options, from historic homes with character to modern new builds. Home prices vary depending on location and size, but the area consistently sees strong demand due to its amenities and location.

Things to Do

  • Explore the shops and restaurants in downtown Glen Ellyn
  • Relax or kayak at Lake Ellyn
  • Attend the Glen Ellyn Festival of the Arts
  • Take advantage of the Illinois Prairie Path for biking and walking
