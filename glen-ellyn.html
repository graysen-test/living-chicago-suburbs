Welcome to Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is a charming village in DuPage County, Illinois, known for its tree-lined streets, historic downtown, and vibrant community atmosphere. With a blend of historic architecture and modern amenities, Glen Ellyn offers a perfect balance of suburban comfort and city accessibility.

From its scenic Lake Ellyn to the bustling shops and restaurants along Main Street, Glen Ellyn offers residents and visitors a warm and welcoming experience year-round.
