About Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is known for its charming downtown, beautiful parks, and strong sense of community. Residents enjoy a mix of historic homes, modern conveniences, and top-rated schools. The village offers plenty of recreational activities, from boating at Lake Ellyn to browsing boutiques and cafes in the heart of town.

Community Highlights

  • Population: ~28,000
  • Top-Rated Schools in District 41 & 87
  • Metra Access to Downtown Chicago
  • Lake Ellyn Park and Village Links Golf Course
  • Vibrant Downtown with Local Shops & Restaurants
