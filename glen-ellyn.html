Welcome to Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is a picturesque village located just 23 miles west of downtown Chicago. Known for its charming downtown, top-rated schools, and strong sense of community, Glen Ellyn offers the perfect blend of suburban comfort and urban convenience.

With a vibrant mix of boutiques, restaurants, and cafes, the downtown area is a hub for both locals and visitors. Outdoor enthusiasts enjoy Lake Ellyn Park, the Illinois Prairie Path, and numerous green spaces throughout the village.

Glen Ellyn’s real estate market is diverse, offering everything from historic homes with character to modern new builds. Its commuter-friendly Metra station makes it a popular choice for professionals working in the city while enjoying a quieter lifestyle.

Whether you’re drawn to its historic charm, excellent schools, or active community life, Glen Ellyn remains one of the most desirable suburbs in the Chicago area.
