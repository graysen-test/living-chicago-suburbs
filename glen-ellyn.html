Welcome to Glen Ellyn

Glen Ellyn is the “Village of Volunteers,” known for its tree-full streets, historic downtown, Lake Ellyn, and close-knit community feel — all within a stone’s throw of Chicago.

With its walkable downtown, favorite local eateries, and local events like the Fourth of July parade and Friday farmers’ market, Glen Ellyn brings that small-town warmth your clients will love.
