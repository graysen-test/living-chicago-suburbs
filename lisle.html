Lisle

Lisle is a welcoming suburb located about 25 miles west of Chicago, known for its green spaces, community spirit, and convenient location.

Community & Lifestyle

Home to the Morton Arboretum and beautiful neighborhoods, Lisle offers a perfect blend of suburban calm and easy access to the city.

Schools

Lisle Community Unit School District 202 and nearby Naperville District 203 serve the area with strong educational programs.

Real Estate

Lisle features a mix of charming single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums, many near parks and nature trails.

Things to Do

  • Explore the Morton Arboretum
  • Attend Eyes to the Skies Festival
  • Enjoy community parks and trails
  • Shop and dine in the revitalized downtown area
