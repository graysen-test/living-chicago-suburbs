Meet Brandon Blankenship

Hi, I’m Brandon! I grew up right here in Glen Ellyn, and now I get to help people find their homes all across DuPage County — from Naperville and Wheaton to Elmhurst, Hinsdale, and beyond.

I started Living Chicago Suburbs to give people a real feel for what life is like in the western suburbs — the neighborhoods, the schools, the local spots that make each town unique. Along the way, the community really grew. Today, over 90,000 people follow along on Instagram to see what’s happening in the suburbs.

I’ve been honored to be named to REALTORS® 30 Under 30 and, over the last five years, I’ve helped close 169 sales totaling $68.6 million, with homes up to $1.7 million.

But numbers aren’t really the point — for me, it’s about helping people make the right move for their life, whether that’s buying your first home in Naperville, upsizing in Wheaton, or relocating to Glen Ellyn.

If you’re thinking about making a move in DuPage County, I’d love to help.
