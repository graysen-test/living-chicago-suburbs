Meet Brandon Blankenship

Brandon Blankenship is one of the Western Suburbs’ most trusted and accomplished real estate professionals. Born and raised in Glen Ellyn, Brandon offers clients a deep, first-hand understanding of the area’s communities, schools, and lifestyle.

Named to the prestigious 30 Under 30 by the National Association of REALTORS®, Brandon has closed 169 sales totaling $68.6 million over the past five years, representing homes up to $1.7 million. With over 90,000 Instagram followers on his Living Chicago Suburbs platform, his reach and expertise extend far beyond the local market.

Whether you’re buying your first home, upgrading, or relocating, Brandon combines local knowledge, top-tier marketing, and skilled negotiation to help you achieve your goals.
