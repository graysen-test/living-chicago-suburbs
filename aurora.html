Aurora

Aurora, Illinois is the second-largest city in the state, known for its diverse community, rich history, and vibrant arts scene.

Community & Lifestyle

Nicknamed the “City of Lights,” Aurora offers cultural attractions, beautiful riverfront areas, and a growing downtown district.

Schools

Aurora is served by multiple school districts, including West Aurora District 129 and East Aurora District 131, along with acclaimed private schools.

Real Estate

Housing in Aurora ranges from historic homes to modern developments, offering options for a variety of lifestyles and budgets.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Paramount Theatre
  • Explore Phillips Park Zoo
  • Enjoy the Fox River Trail
  • Attend RiverEdge Park concerts
