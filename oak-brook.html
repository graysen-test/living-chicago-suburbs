Oak Brook

Oak Brook is a prestigious suburb located about 19 miles west of Chicago, known for its luxury homes, corporate headquarters, and upscale shopping and dining experiences.

Community & Lifestyle

The village offers a blend of residential tranquility and high-end amenities. Oakbrook Center Mall is one of the largest shopping centers in the Chicago area, and the community hosts various cultural and recreational events throughout the year.

Schools

Oak Brook is served by several highly regarded school districts, including Butler District 53 and Hinsdale Central High School, both recognized for academic excellence.

Real Estate

The area features expansive estates, luxury townhomes, and gated communities. Its prime location and amenities make it one of the most desirable suburbs in the region.

Things to Do

  • Shop at Oakbrook Center
  • Golf at the Oak Brook Golf Club
  • Explore Fullersburg Woods Forest Preserve
  • Dine at award-winning restaurants
