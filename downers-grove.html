Downers Grove

Downers Grove is a vibrant community located about 22 miles west of Chicago, known for its charming downtown, excellent schools, and convenient commuter access via Metra and major highways.

Community & Lifestyle

The downtown area is a hub of activity, with coffee shops, restaurants, and seasonal events like the Downers Grove Farmers Market. The townâ€™s many parks and trails make it a great choice for active lifestyles.

Schools

Downers Grove is served by multiple highly rated school districts, including District 58 for elementary/middle schools and District 99 for high schools.

Real Estate

Housing options range from historic homes near downtown to newer builds in surrounding neighborhoods, offering a variety of price points for families and professionals.

Things to Do

  • Shop and dine along Main Street
  • Catch a movie at the historic Tivoli Theatre
  • Enjoy outdoor activities at Lyman Woods
  • Attend summer concerts in the park
