Why Downers Grove

Known for its mature trees, downtown charm, and commuter convenience, Downers Grove offers a strong community feel and diverse housing stock with excellent access to Metra and expressways.

Signature Neighborhoods

Downtown / Main St. Pierce Downer North Downers Denburn Woods Belle Aire
  • Downtown/Main: Walkable, historic homes + new infill, dining and boutique retail.
  • Denburn Woods: Wooded enclave, close to parks and schools.

Schools

Served by Community High School District 99 and several strong elementary districts; a range of academic, arts, and athletics options.

Lifestyle & Amenities

  • Downtown festivals, farmers market, and classic car nights
  • Extensive park system and Lyman Woods Nature Center
  • Multiple Metra BNSF stops for easy Chicago access

Housing Market Overview

From charming bungalows and mid-century homes to new construction near downtown; townhomes and condos provide lock-and-leave options.

Local Tips from Brandon

  • Proximity to Metra and downtown typically drives demand — plan tours accordingly.
  • Watch for infill construction opportunities on larger lots north of the tracks.
