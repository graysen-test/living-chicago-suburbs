Why Naperville

Naperville blends a vibrant downtown, award-winning schools, and 130+ parks with a welcoming, community-first feel. It’s consistently recognized among the nation’s best places to live, with walkable amenities and a thriving restaurant scene anchored by the Riverwalk.

Signature Neighborhoods

Downtown Naperville East Highlands Cress Creek Ashbury White Eagle Brookdale
  • Downtown: Historic homes + townhomes, walk-to shops/dining, Riverwalk access.
  • White Eagle: Golf community, larger lots, pool/club amenities.
  • Ashbury: Family-friendly, community pool, trails, parks.

Schools

Primarily served by Naperville 203 and parts of Indian Prairie 204, both highly regarded districts with strong AP offerings, athletics, and music programs.

Lifestyle & Amenities

  • Riverwalk, Centennial Beach, and extensive park systems
  • Commuter rail access to Chicago via Metra BNSF
  • Downtown shopping, dining, and events year-round

Housing Market Overview

Single-family homes, townhomes, and condos across a wide range — from starter homes to luxury properties in golf and lake communities.

Local Tips from Brandon

  • Proximity to downtown vs. school boundaries can shift pricing quickly — align both with your goals.
  • Golf and pool communities book fast for tours in spring; plan ahead for weekend showings.
