Naperville

Naperville is one of the largest and most vibrant suburbs of Chicago, located about 28 miles west of the city. Known for its award-winning schools, thriving downtown, and strong community spirit, Naperville offers a high quality of life and plenty of activities for residents and visitors alike.

Community & Lifestyle

Naperville’s Riverwalk is the city’s crown jewel, offering scenic pathways, fountains, and recreational areas along the DuPage River. The city is home to a variety of cultural events, family-friendly festivals, and a bustling dining and shopping scene that caters to all tastes.

Schools

Naperville is served by some of the top-ranked school districts in Illinois, including Naperville Community Unit School District 203 and Indian Prairie School District 204, making it a top choice for families.

Real Estate

From charming historic homes near downtown to sprawling modern estates and new construction communities, Naperville’s real estate market offers something for everyone. Home values remain strong due to the city’s excellent amenities and reputation.

Things to Do

  • Stroll along the Naperville Riverwalk
  • Visit Centennial Beach for summer fun
  • Explore the shops and restaurants in downtown Naperville
  • Attend the Naperville Independent Film Festival
  • Discover local history at Naper Settlement
