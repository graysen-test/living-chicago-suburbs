Elmhurst

Elmhurst is a bustling suburb located about 17 miles west of Chicago, known for its beautiful tree-lined streets, strong sense of community, and thriving arts scene.

Community & Lifestyle

Residents enjoy a mix of historic charm and modern convenience, with a vibrant downtown featuring boutiques, restaurants, and cultural attractions such as the Elmhurst Art Museum.

Schools

Elmhurst is served by District 205, which is highly rated for academic performance and extracurricular opportunities.

Real Estate

The housing market includes charming bungalows, mid-century homes, and newer luxury builds, appealing to families and professionals alike.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Elmhurst Art Museum
  • Relax at Wilder Park
  • Shop and dine in downtown Elmhurst
  • Attend community festivals throughout the year
