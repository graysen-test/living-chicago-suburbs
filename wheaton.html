Wheaton

Wheaton is a charming suburb located about 25 miles west of Chicago, known for its strong community spirit, beautiful parks, and top-rated schools. It combines a welcoming small-town feel with easy access to big-city amenities.

Community & Lifestyle

Downtown Wheaton offers boutique shopping, diverse dining options, and a variety of seasonal events. Cantigny Park and the Wheaton Public Library are local favorites, and residents enjoy a wealth of cultural and recreational opportunities year-round.

Schools

Wheaton is served by Community Unit School District 200, which has a reputation for academic excellence and a variety of extracurricular programs.

Real Estate

Housing options range from historic homes with character to newer developments and condominiums. The real estate market remains strong due to Wheaton’s excellent schools and family-friendly amenities.

Things to Do

  • Explore Cantigny Park’s gardens and museums
  • Attend events at Wheaton College
  • Shop and dine in downtown Wheaton
  • Hike the Illinois Prairie Path
